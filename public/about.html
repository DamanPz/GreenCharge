About Green Charge

A sustainable, solar-powered EV charging initiative for Bengaluru.

What is Green Charge?

Green Charge is a smart solar-powered electric vehicle charging station network designed to support clean energy adoption in Bengaluru. The system provides real-time visibility of charging stations, availability status, and capacity, promoting sustainable urban mobility.

The Problem We Address

  • Rising EV adoption with limited charging infrastructure
  • Dependency on non-renewable energy sources
  • Lack of visibility into station availability and usage

Our Solution

  • Solar-powered charging stations
  • Real-time station tracking
  • Admin dashboard for station management
  • Interactive map-based station discovery

Aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goals

SDG 7 – Affordable and Clean Energy
Promoting renewable energy usage and sustainable infrastructure.
